Singapore Zoo
‍

Located in the northwestern part of the island, the Singapore Zoo is a haven for a variety of marvelous wildlife and a must-see attraction for animal lovers.This popular zoo first opened its doors to the public in 1973 and since then has catapulted to become one of the best rainforest zoos in the world.Not only is the zoo home to over 2,800 animals from 300 species, including crocodiles, Malayan tapirs and white tigers, but it has also bagged numerous international and local awards.
