Jurong Bird Park
‍

Nature lovers and like-minded individuals come together at the Singapore Bird Park to unleash their fascination with the world of birds. With the closure of Jurong Bird Park in 2023, Singapore Bird Park opens its doors to a new life. The new Bird of Prey Park is housed at Wildlife World, an integrated wildlife destination and home to the Singapore Zoo, Night Safari and River Ecology Park.Highlights include eight walk-in aviaries, each reflecting habitats from around the world, such as lush African rainforests, South American wetlands, Southeast Asian rice paddies, and dry eucalyptus forests in Australia.
